Step 5 of 6
The Agape standard.
Every person on our network agrees to these principles. They're not rules — they're the reason our clients trust us in their homes.
⏰
Always on timeGPS check-in is required on arrival. Consistent late check-ins affect your tier standing. If you're running late, notify through the app — always.
📋
Complete the full checklistEvery task, every room, every job. Partial completions are flagged. If something genuinely can't be done, mark it N/A with a note — never skip silently.
📸
Photo documentationMinimum 6 photos per clean job. Photos protect you as much as they protect the client. Damage found on arrival must be photographed immediately.
🔒
Total discretionWhat you see in a client's home stays there. No photos of personal belongings, no social media, no discussion of client details with anyone outside Agape.
✨
The Agape signature touchOur premium clients expect more than clean — they expect considered. The folded toilet paper, the welcome card, the arranged pillows. These details are why they pay for Agape.
📱
App-first communicationAll job communication happens through the Agape app — not personal text or calls. This protects you, the client, and ensures Agape can support any issue.
